About Ace Academy

Ace Academy is an online school in collaboration with the Telangana Government Organization.

We have experienced and qualified tutors who mentor each student according to their individual capabilities. The school operates from Sunday to Thursday, with weekends dedicated to checkpoint worksheets. Our 1:1 teacher-student ratio ensures personalized attention and minimizes distractions.

We follow the British Curriculum for students up to Grade 5. From Grade 6 onwards, we use the books published by the State Council of Educational Research and Training (SCERT) of Telangana. Telugu is a compulsory subject, with an option to choose between Urdu and Hindi.

Our curriculum is designed to enhance students' growth by prioritizing and creating more opportunities for learning. Parents can choose between tuition-free and tuition-based online school options, both offering the same high standards and benefits.

Ace Academy is more than just an online school; it's a partner in your child's educational journey. Together with our experienced tutors, supportive community, and comprehensive curriculum, we aim to unlock your child's full potential and set them on a path to success.
